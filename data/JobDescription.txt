Job Title: Data Scientist & Analytics Specialist
Company: InnovateTech Solutions
Location: Remote / Hybrid - San Francisco, CA

About the Company:
InnovateTech Solutions is a fast-growing tech company focused on delivering AI-powered analytics and business intelligence tools for the retail and finance sectors. We help clients leverage data to optimize operations, improve customer experience, and drive revenue growth.

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Data Scientist & Analytics Specialist to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in data analytics, machine learning, and business intelligence, with the ability to translate complex data into actionable insights.

Key Responsibilities:

Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for business improvements.

Develop and deploy predictive and prescriptive machine learning models to solve real-world business problems.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams including product, engineering, and marketing to support data-driven decision making.

Build interactive dashboards and reports using BI tools such as Power BI or Tableau for internal and client use.

Perform A/B testing, statistical analysis, and hypothesis testing to validate model effectiveness and business strategies.

Automate data pipelines and ensure data quality for reliable analytics.

Communicate findings and insights clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Qualifications:

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, or related field.

2+ years of experience in data science, analytics, or a related role.

Strong programming skills in Python and SQL.

Experience with machine learning libraries (e.g., scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch).

Proficiency in data visualization tools like Power BI, Tableau, or similar.

Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) and data engineering tools is a plus.

Excellent communication and presentation skills.

Preferred Skills:

Knowledge of NLP or computer vision techniques.

Experience working in agile development environments.

Understanding of business metrics and KPIs in retail or finance domains.
